    üéØ When to Use Each Indicator

    Use Pentarch When...

    • ‚úÖ You want to identify market cycle phase
    • ‚úÖ Looking for early-cycle (TD, IGN) or late-cycle (WRN, CAP, BDN) signals
    • ‚úÖ Day trading or swing trading volatile assets
    • ‚ùå Avoid: Scalping <15m, low volatility periods

    Use Janus Atlas When...

    • ‚úÖ You need specific price levels for entries/exits
    • ‚úÖ Setting stop losses and take profits
    • ‚úÖ Any trading style, any timeframe
    • ‚ùå Avoid: Parabolic moves, newly listed assets

    Use Omnideck When...

    • ‚úÖ You want comprehensive 8-system analysis
    • ‚úÖ You're an advanced trader with 100+ hours to invest
    • ‚úÖ You understand TD Sequential, VWAP, oscillators
    • ‚ùå Avoid: Beginners, visual complexity intolerance

    Use Augury Grid When...

    • ‚úÖ You trade multiple assets and need daily screening
    • ‚úÖ Morning market scan to find best setups
    • ‚úÖ Crypto altcoin trading, sector rotation analysis
    • ‚ùå Avoid: Single-asset focus, position traders

    Use Volume Oracle When...

    • ‚úÖ You need volume confirmation for patterns/levels
    • ‚úÖ Identifying breakout vs fake-out
    • ‚úÖ Tracking open position P&L
    • ‚ùå Avoid: Spot forex (use futures), very long timeframes

    Use Harmonic Oscillator When...

    • ‚úÖ You want real-time momentum confirmation
    • ‚úÖ Need to know if market is overbought/oversold
    • ‚úÖ Filtering setups with momentum agreement
    • ‚ùå Avoid: Very long timeframes (too noisy)

    Use Plutus Flow When...

    • ‚úÖ You want to spot price-volume divergences
    • ‚úÖ Identifying accumulation/distribution
    • ‚úÖ Swing trading reversal setups
    • ‚ùå Avoid: Scalping, spot forex (use futures)
