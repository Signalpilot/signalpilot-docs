    ‚ö° Quick Interpretation Guide

    Early-Cycle Signals (Bullish Potential)

    • TD: Accumulation phase identified ‚Üí Watch for bounce
    • IGN: Markup beginning ‚Üí Momentum shifting bullish

    Late-Cycle Signals (Caution/Reversal Potential)

    • WRN: Distribution detected ‚Üí Consider profit-taking
    • CAP: Climax exhaustion ‚Üí High risk of reversal
    • BDN: Decline initiated ‚Üí Bearish structure confirmed

    ü§ù Best Combinations

    The Trinity (Highest Probability)

    1. ‚úÖ Pentarch pattern (any of 5 signals)
    2. ‚úÖ Janus Atlas level within 1-2%
    3. ‚úÖ Volume Oracle spike >1.5x

    Example: TD at Daily Low + 2.0x volume = Strong early-cycle setup

    With Harmonic Oscillator

    • IGN + Harmonic BUY/STRONG = Momentum aligned
    • CAP + Harmonic SELL/STRONG = Reversal aligned

    ‚ùå Sub-5-minute charts (too much noise) ‚ùå Low volatility/tight consolidation ‚ùå Low volume assets (<$500K daily) ‚ùå 30 min before/after major news (FOMC, NFP)

    üîß Troubleshooting

    "I'm not seeing signals"

    • Switch to 1H/4H/Daily charts
    • Check if market is consolidating (normal)
    • Test on BTC, SPY, EUR/USD first

    "Too many signals"

    • Increase to higher timeframe
    • This is normal during high volatility

    "Signals disappear mid-bar"

    • This is normal! Signals confirm at bar close only
    • Set alerts to "Once Per Bar Close"

    üéØ Pattern Success Tips

    1. Observe 20-30 signals before trading
    2. Combine with levels (Janus) + volume (Oracle)
    3. Start on Daily charts (clearest)
    4. TD alone is weak; TD+level+volume is strong
    5. Not every TD leads to IGN (incomplete cycles are normal)
